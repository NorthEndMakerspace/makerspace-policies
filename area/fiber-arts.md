- Most sewing machines, all sergers, and all other tools do not require certification to use.
- The [industrial sewing machine (Juki LU-562)](https://nemakers.notion.site/Juki-LU-562-industrial-sewing-machine-194f0d96021080d6a8f3c34bf081433e) can be dangerious and thus requires special training to use. 
- The [computerized embroidery machine (Husqvarna)](https://nemakers.notion.site/Husqvarna-Viking-Designer-Diamond-DeLuxe-embroidery-machine-e5ccd2278ec64723a46195feeb3258c5) does not require certification. It operates similarly to a normal sewing machine, but has additional steps to set up materials and digitize designs.

The rules in [All areas]({% link area/all-areas.md %}) apply.

## General rules

- Discard scraps smaller than 1 sq ft.
- Fabric scissors must only be used on fabric or thread. Do not cut paper or any other materials with them.

## Safety

- Do not leave a plugged-in iron unattended.

## Before leaving

- **Sewing machines**: remove and put away the bobbin and the spool.
- **Sergers**: leave the serger threaded and ready for use.

Remember to clean up any fabric or thread scapes, put away clips, pins, bobbins, etc.
